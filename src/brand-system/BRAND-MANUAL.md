## 1. THE KINETIC PALETTE

## 3. LOGO GEOMETRY
- **The Monolithic Gap:** Exactly 8px between the icon and typography.
- **Scaling:** Baseline height must be 32px, 48px, or 64px to maintain pixel-perfect rendering.
- **Master File:** Pull from `components/Branding/RefinedLogo.tsx`.
